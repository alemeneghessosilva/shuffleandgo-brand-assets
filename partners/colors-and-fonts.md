# Colors & Fonts

## Color palette

The brand runs on **four colors only**. Stick to these — adding a fifth dilutes the system.

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | HSL | CMYK | Pantone Solid Coated | Pantone Alt |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Primary (the "brand black") | Deep Forest Green | `#223728` | 34, 55, 40 | 137°, 24%, 17% | 38, 0, 27, 78 | 5535 C | 5605 C |
| Accent (the one thing) | Aged Gold | `#be9a50` | 190, 154, 80 | 40°, 46%, 53% | 0, 19, 58, 25 | 7563 C | 871 C (metallic) |
| Body on light | Charcoal | `#575756` | 87, 87, 86 | 60°, 1%, 34% | 0, 0, 1, 66 | 425 C | Cool Gray 11 C |
| Body on dark | Cream | `#f4e4a9` | 244, 228, 169 | 47°, 77%, 81% | 0, 7, 31, 4 | 7401 C | 7499 C |

### Usage share (target)

- Forest Green: **~80%** of any composition (the background, the dominant surface).
- Aged Gold: **~10%** — never wallpaper. One headline word, one badge, one CTA. Pick one.
- Charcoal: ~5% (body on light surfaces).
- Cream: ~5% (subtitles, soft secondary copy on green).

### Contrast pairs to know

- **Cream on Forest** — ratio 10.1 (AAA). Default body / subtitle on dark. **Preferred.**
- **Gold on Forest** — ratio 4.8 (AA-large). Headline / accent on dark. Display only.
- **Charcoal on Cream** — ratio 5.7 (AA). Preferred body on light.
- **Forest on Cream** — ratio 10.1 (AAA). Headline on light.

### Pairs to **avoid**

- **Gold on Cream** — ratio 2.1 — fails contrast. Don't pair these directly. If unavoidable, add a Forest or Charcoal outline.
- **Gold on Charcoal** — ratio 2.7 — fails. Use Forest as the dark surface instead.

## Typography

| Role | Family | Weights | License | Min size |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | **Cabinet Grotesk** | 700–900 | ITF Free Font License (commercial OK) | 14 pt |
| Body | **Inter** | 400–500 | OFL | — |
| Mono / data | **JetBrains Mono** | 400–500 | OFL | numeric data only |

### Fallbacks (when Cabinet Grotesk can't ship — e.g., Google Docs, Notion, image-gen APIs)

- Display: **Inter Display 800**.
- Body: system-ui sans-serif.

### Hard rules

- **ALL CAPS for display impact only.** Sentence case in body. Never shout in body copy.
- Subtitles: **tracked small caps, +150 to +200 letter-spacing, in Aged Gold.**
- Cabinet Grotesk under 14pt → switch to Inter.
- Never use slab serifs, novelty display fonts, hand-letter approximations, or "gamer" fonts.

### Where to get them

- Cabinet Grotesk: https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/cabinet-grotesk
- Inter: https://rsms.me/inter/
- JetBrains Mono: https://www.jetbrains.com/mono/
